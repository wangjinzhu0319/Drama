  • 《Ghost》

    GHOST is a timeless fantasy about the power of love. Walking back to their apartment one night, Sam and Molly are mugged, leaving Sam murdered on a dark street. Sam is trapped as a ghost between this world and the next and unable to leave Molly who he learns is in grave danger. With the help of a phony storefront psychic, Oda Mae Brown, Sam tries to communicate with Molly in the hope of saving and protecting her.

  • 《Cirkopolis》

    Founded in 1993, Cirque Eloize expresses its innovative nature through theatricality and humanity, and combines circus arts with music, dance and theatre in a path-breaking and original manner. With its constant focus on artistic research, the organization ranks among the leading contemporary circuses. With ten original productions to its credit, Cirque Eloize has presented over 4,000 performances in 440 cities in 40 different countries.
    In addition to its touring shows. Cirque Eloize develops custom-made concepts for international special events. To date, more than 1300 events have taken place.

  • 《The Bodyguard》

    "Former Secret Service agent turned bodyguard, Frank Farmer, is hired to protect superstar Rachel Marron from an unknown stalker. Each expects to be in charge – what they don't expect is to fall in love."
    A ‘BRILLIANT!’ (The Times), breathtakingly romantic thriller, THE BODYGUARD features a whole host of irresistible classics including Queen of the Night, So Emotional, One Moment in Time, Saving All My Love, Run to You, I Have Nothing, Greatest Love Of All, Million Dollar Bill, I Wanna Dance With Somebody and one of the greatest hit songs of all time – I Will Always Love You

About Shanghai Fresh Vogur Limited

Shanghai Fresh Vogur Limited was founded in 1996. In its early days, Shanghai Fresh Vogur focused on importing and releasing video products from abroad in China, such as Luc Besson’s Léon, Subway, etc. Meanwhile, the company imported many DVDs of musical and concert, such as Hey Mr. Producer! The Musical World of Cameron Mackintosh, Les Miserables: The 10th Anniversary Dream Cast in Concert at London's Royal Albert Hall, Smoky Joe’s Café, etc. And it produced the first TV program introducing musical to Chinese audiences, Colorful Musicals, for 52 episodes.
In 2009, Shanghai Fresh Vogur worked as copresenter for musical Cats in Shanghai Majestic Theater. From 2014 to 2016, Shanghai Fresh Vogur invested and produced Ghost the Musical tour in the Asianacific region. The musical has toured 6 cities in China for 62 shows in 2015. Additionally, it toured in Australia in 4 cities for 117 shows. Shanghai Fresh Vogur will tour Cirque Éloize Cirkopolis in 20 cities for 38 shows in China in 2016.
